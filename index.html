Kanika Jain, Writer

About

Kanika Jain is an undergraduate student from Mumbai. Her work has appeared in Indian as well as international publications such as The Hindu, Huffington Post, and Man Repeller. She has also worked in marketing and writing creative content for education. Kanika studied at HR College in Mumbai and has interned at firms such as MyGlamm, Talerang, Saatchi and Saatchi, and LaughGuru as a writer and marketing intern. She also worked on the promotion of the book 'Career Rules' by Sonya Dutta Choudhary.



Awards

Kanika has won many words for her writing. These include winning the Tata Litfest creative writing contest for a story.
